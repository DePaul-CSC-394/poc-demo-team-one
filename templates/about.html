Who We Are

UniVerse is a platform designed to make student life easier by connecting students with the housing, supplies, and roommates they need. Whether you're looking for a place to stay, renting out your own space, or searching for the perfect roommate, UniVerse provides a seamless experience tailored to student needs.

Find & List Houses for Rent

Discover the perfect place to stay or list your own property for rent. Our platform connects students with housing options that suit their needs.

Find & List Supplies to Rent

Rent or list supplies such as textbooks, electronics, and more. We make it easy for students to find what they need or rent out their own items.

Match with Roommates

Connect with other students to find the perfect roommate based on your preferences. Our matching system ensures a seamless experience.
